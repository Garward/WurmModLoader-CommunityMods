## Power Scaling - Number Go Up Simulator

**THIS IS THE SECRET SAUCE** 🌶️

Power Scaling makes BOTH you AND monsters scale exponentially. This is how we create the **challenge → innovation → victory** gameplay loop.

### Your Power Level (Main Character Energy)

Your power comes from **FOUR sources**:

1. **Base Power** = Your character level × 1.0
   - Level 50 character = 50 base power
   - Level 100 character = 100 base power
   - Math is hard but this one's easy

2. **Kill Power** = Total kills × 0.01 (with diminishing returns)
   - First 1000 kills: FULL XP GAINZ
   - After 1000 kills: Diminishing returns kick in
   - Formula: `1.0 / (1.0 + (kills / 1000))`
   - Translation: Go fight HARDER enemies, not more trash mobs

3. **Achievement Power** = Bonus from achievements
   - Kill a dragon? +10 power
   - Solo a troll king? +15 power
   - Touch grass? -5 power (jk, go outside sometime)

4. **Quest Power** = Bonus from quest completion
   - Not implemented yet (SOON™)
   - Imagine reputation grinds but they actually make you STRONK

### The Power Curve (Exponential Copium)

**Every 25 power levels = 2x combat effectiveness**

```
Power 1-10:   Normal Wurm gameplay (humble beginnings)
Power 25:     2x damage/defense (starting to feel it)
Power 50:     4x damage/defense (we're GAMING now)
Power 75:     8x damage/defense (who needs a party?)
Power 100:    16x damage/defense (you are the raid boss)
Power 125:    32x damage/defense (god has left the server)
```

**Translation:** At max power, you hit **32 TIMES HARDER** than when you started.

No. Cap. 🚫🧢

### Combat Scaling (The Juicy Math)

When you hit something:

```
Your Damage = Base Damage × (1.0 + PowerLevel × 0.02)
Your Defense = Base Defense × (1.0 + PowerLevel × 0.01)
Your HP = Base HP × (1.0 + PowerLevel × 0.05)
```

**What this means:**
- Power 100 = **+200% damage** 💥
- Power 100 = **+100% defense** 🛡️
- Power 100 = **+500% HP** ❤️

You become an **ABSOLUTE UNIT**.

### Monster Power (The Catch)

**HERE'S THE TWIST:** Monsters ALSO have power levels.

```
Monster Power = CR × Type Multiplier

Type Multipliers:
├─ Regular mob: 1.0x   (standard fare)
├─ Champion: 2.0x      (mini-boss energy)
├─ Unique: 5.0x        (BOSS FIGHT)
└─ Titan: 10.0x        (custom super bosses, GG)
```

**Why this is BASED:**
- Early game: You're stronger than mobs (power fantasy engaged)
- Mid game: Mobs catch up (gotta optimize your build)
- Late game: High-tier mobs are CRACKED (git gud or get rekt)

This creates **meaningful progression**. You can't just face-tank everything. You need:
- Soulbound weapon (+40% damage at level 20) ✓
- Material infusion (elemental advantage) ✓
- Power level grinding (kill count go up) ✓
- SKILL (jk just bring more gear) ✓

### The Meta Implications

**Power Level 25:** You can solo champions comfortably
**Power Level 50:** You can duo uniques
**Power Level 75:** You can solo uniques (gigachad status)
**Power Level 100:** You ARE the raid boss

**BUT** if you try to fight a Titan (power 500+) at level 50?

## 🩹 Healing System - "Why Am I Not Dead Yet?"

**The Problem (Vanilla Wurm):**
Kill 1 mob → bandage for 5 minutes → wait 10 more minutes → kill another → repeat → uninstall game

**The Solution (Power Fantasy Mod):**
HEALING THAT DOESN'T SUCK + LOCATION MATTERS

### How It Works (The Two Systems)

**System 1: Sanctuary Aura (Out of Combat)**
- Don't get hit for 10 seconds → Sanctuary activates
- Heals **smallest wounds first** (feels good watching them disappear)
- Every 5 seconds: Apply healing
- BUT HERE'S THE TWIST: **WHERE YOU ARE MATTERS**

**System 2: Soul Drain (Lifesteal)**
- Kill enemy with soulbound weapon → instant heal
- Heals **largest wounds first** (keeps you alive in combat)
- 2 second cooldown (can't cheese with chickens)

---

### Location-Based Healing (THE STRATEGIC LAYER)

**Your Village:** 🏠
- **2.0x healing multiplier**
- Power 50: Full heal in ~10 seconds
- Safe, sustainable grinding
- **Build your home base for POWER SPIKE**

**Near Guard Tower:** 🗼
- **1.5x healing multiplier**
- Power 50: Full heal in ~15 seconds
- Safer frontier exploration
- **Extend your safe zone with towers**

**Wilderness:** 🌲
- **1.0x healing multiplier** (standard)
- Power 50: Full heal in ~20 seconds
- Slower, need to be careful
- **Risk vs reward: push deeper or retreat?**

**Enemy Village:** ⚔️
- **0.5x healing multiplier** (HOSTILE)
- Power 50: Full heal in ~40 seconds
- HIGH RISK raiding
- **Extract quickly or die**

**Enemy Deed:** 💀
- **0.0x healing multiplier** (NO REGEN)
- Power 50: **ZERO HEALING**
- Hardcore mode
- **Lifesteal only, GIT GUD**
