## Meme Tier Classification

### Why "Meme Worthy"
1. **Absurd Damage**: 19k base damage is ridiculous
2. **Extreme Commitment**: 8 second swing timer - you're locked in!
3. **High Risk**: Reach 2 means you must get close
4. **Poor Defense**: 30% parry = can't defend well
5. **Comedy Factor**: Oversized club doing 2x damage of huge axe

### Why "Balanced"
1. **DPS Similar to Huge Axe**: 2.38 vs 2.41
2. **Heavy Stamina Drain**: Weight group 5
3. **Tactical Weapon**: Good for hit-and-run, not sustained combat
4. **Short Reach Penalty**: Must position dangerously
5. **Skill Required**: Miss = 8 second vulnerability

## Playstyle

**Perfect For:**
- Drive-by one-shots
- Ambush attacks
- Tank busters (high burst on slow targets)
- Comedic "oversized weapon" roleplay
- Players who enjoy high-risk high-reward

**Terrible For:**
- Sustained combat (DPS slightly lower than huge axe)
- Mobile enemies (8s swing timer + reach 2)
- Defensive play (30% parry)
- Low stamina builds (weight 5)

## Server Impact

**PvE:**
- One-shots most normal mobs
- Good against slow/stationary bosses
- Inefficient for grinding (low DPS)

**PvP (with 0.7x multiplier):**
- ~13,300 damage @ Q100 (still devastating)
- High burst for alpha strikes
- Vulnerable during 8s cooldown
- Tactical weapon, not spam weapon
